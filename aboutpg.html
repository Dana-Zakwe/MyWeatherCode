About the Progress Monitoring System

This system is designed to help learners and teachers track academic activities, submissions, and progress. It offers a user-friendly interface with dynamic features to support students at different levels.

  • Home
  • Login
  • Dashboard

Objective:

A comprehensive, user-friendly, and secure web platform to facilitate efficient tutoring and progress tracking, enhancing the educational experience for students, teachers, and administrators.

Specific Objectives:

For Students:

  • Easy access to tutoring schedules and materials
  • Personalized learning plans and progress tracking
  • Interactive tools for engagement and feedback
  • Secure messaging system for communication with teachers

For Teachers:

  • Streamlined tutoring scheduling and management
  • Automated progress tracking and reporting
  • Access to student learning plans and materials
  • Tools for creating and assigning interactive content

For Administrators:

  • Centralized dashboard for monitoring progress
  • Automated reporting and analytics
  • User management and access control
  • Integration with existing school management systems

Key Functionalities:

  • User registration and login
  • Tutoring scheduling and management
  • Progress tracking and analytics
